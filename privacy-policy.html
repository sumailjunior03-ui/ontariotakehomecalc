1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ontariotakehomecalc.ca ("we", "our", or "this site") collects, uses, and handles information when you visit our website. It also describes your rights regarding that information and how to exercise them.

This site is operated as part of the calc-hq.ca Canadian calculator network. It is a fully static website hosted on Cloudflare Pages with no backend server, no database, and no user account system of any kind. By using this website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

This policy is governed by the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and the privacy laws of Ontario, Canada.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information through this website's own systems. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any other personal identifier
  • Your income, salary, pay frequency, or any financial figures you enter into the calculator
  • Device fingerprints, browser identifiers, or hardware information
  • Location data beyond the general region inferred by third-party ad networks (see Section 5)
  • Server-side form submissions — any contact form on this site routes through a third-party service

All calculator inputs and outputs are processed entirely within your browser using JavaScript. No data you enter is ever transmitted to any server operated by us. When you close or refresh the page, all entered values are permanently discarded.

3. Information Automatically Collected by Third Parties

While we do not directly collect personal data, certain third-party services embedded on this site may automatically collect information about your visit through standard web technology — HTTP requests, cookies, and tracking pixels. This collection is governed entirely by the respective third party's own privacy policies, not ours. The third parties present on this site are Google (AdSense advertising) and Cloudflare (hosting and CDN infrastructure), each described in detail below.

4. Cookies

This site does not set any first-party cookies. We do not use cookies for analytics, session management, user preferences, or visitor tracking of any kind.

However, Google AdSense — which serves advertising on this site — sets third-party cookies on your device. These cookies allow Google to serve ads relevant to your interests, measure ad performance, and limit repetitive ad exposure. Google's advertising cookies may track activity across websites that display Google ads, not just this site.

You can manage your Google ad personalization settings at adssettings.google.com, or opt out of third-party advertising cookies through the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page. Most browsers also allow you to block or delete cookies through their privacy settings. Blocking cookies will not affect calculator functionality.
